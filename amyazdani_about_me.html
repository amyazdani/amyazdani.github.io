About Me

Hi, I am Ameneh.

I am a Digital Product Designer. Before that, I belonged to the world of architecture where I enhanced the interaction between humans and the surrounding environment and gave identity and attraction to empty spaces.
By learning design thinking in architecture, I strengthened my skills of analyzing challenges and solving them.

My interest in technology made the topic of my thesis to be the design of a startup environment called "StartArt".
In this project, I interviewed the target users by analyzing their needs, and linking them to the principles of design, in a way to prepare the information architecture for the initial sketch and then iteration until the final design.

During my studies, I spent 3.5 years working in a startup team as a software tester. My responsibilities included evaluating the performance and quality of digital products, as well as the user-product relationship. Through testing and reporting issues and flaws, I provided suggestions to enhance software performance and visual identity.

My educational and professional background, and studies, along with the courses I have passed, have transformed me into a UI/UX designer so that I can help the interaction between people and digital products and act as a mentor within design teams.

Furthermore, to maintain balance in my personal and professional life, I seek help from a therapist and mentor to realize my weaknesses and grow.
